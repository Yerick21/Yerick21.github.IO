About Me

Hello, I’m Yerick L Gonzalez, a passionate web developer and lifelong learner. Currently, I work as a developer at Ozarks Technical Community College, where I focus on creating responsive, user-friendly websites and solving complex technical challenges.

I’m currently expanding my technical skills through courses like Web Development 2, Systems Analysis and Design, and C# 1. These courses are strengthening my expertise in advanced web development concepts, software development methodologies, and object-oriented programming.

My journey started in high school with my robotics team, where teamwork and creativity earned us second place in a competitive tournament. Since then, I’ve built projects like a Student Management System in C#, a responsive pet boarding website, and a travel site for Bali enthusiasts. I also enjoy working on personal projects that combine creativity and technology.

Beyond my coursework and job, I bring a unique mix of creativity and technical knowledge. I love brainstorming ideas for content creation, supporting ethical causes like reducing fast fashion, and working on projects that improve user experience and accessibility. I’m always looking for ways to optimize performance and streamline processes, whether it's in web development, software applications, or problem-solving in general.

As I continue to grow, I aspire to become a full-stack developer, blending front-end design with back-end logic to build seamless digital experiences. I am eager to work in a collaborative environment where I can apply my problem-solving skills, learn from experienced professionals, and contribute to meaningful projects in the tech industry.
