About Me

Hello, I’m Yerick L Gonzalez, a passionate web developer and lifelong learner. Currently, I work as a developer at Ozarks Technical Community College, where I focus on creating responsive, user-friendly websites and solving complex technical challenges.

Over the course of my studies, I’ve taken classes such as Introduction to Programming (C#), Web Development, and Database Management. These have equipped me with a strong foundation in object-oriented programming, responsive design, and working with databases.

My journey started in high school with my robotics team, where teamwork and creativity earned us second place in a competitive tournament. Since then, I’ve built projects like a Student Management System in C#, a responsive pet boarding website, and a travel site for Bali enthusiasts. I also enjoy working on personal projects that combine creativity and technology.

Outside of work and studies, I enjoy brainstorming ideas for funny videos, supporting ethical causes like reducing fast fashion, and experimenting with tech innovations. My ultimate aspiration is to grow as a full-stack developer, combining my technical expertise with creative problem-solving to make meaningful contributions in the IT industry.
