Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for adopting a puppy?

We have a simple adoption process. First, fill out our adoption application. Then, we will schedule an interview to ensure the puppy is a good fit for your family. Once approved, you'll be able to choose a puppy from our available litters.

Are your puppies health-tested?

Yes! All of our puppies are health-tested by a licensed veterinarian before they are placed in their forever homes. We provide health certificates and vaccination records to new owners.

Do you offer training for the puppies?

Yes, we offer basic puppy training courses that include housebreaking, leash training, and basic commands. We also provide tips and resources to help you continue the training at home.

What should I expect when I bring my new puppy home?

Bringing a new puppy home is exciting! We recommend setting up a designated sleeping area, introducing the puppy to your family, and starting the house training process right away. We'll provide you with a checklist to help you prepare for the arrival.
