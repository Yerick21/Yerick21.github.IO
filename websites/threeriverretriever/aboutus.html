About Us

Welcome to Three River Retrievers! We are a family-owned business specializing in breeding top-quality Golden Retrievers. Our mission is to provide healthy, happy, and well-socialized puppies that make perfect companions for families of all sizes.

Our Story

Three River Retrievers was founded with a love for Golden Retrievers and a commitment to responsible breeding. We offer only a few litters per year to ensure that each puppy receives the attention, care, and socialization they need to grow into well-adjusted adults.

Our Mission

We are passionate about providing the best breeding experience for both our puppies and the families who adopt them. Our goal is to raise dogs that are healthy, happy, and have the best temperament. We perform rigorous health testing and follow best practices in dog breeding to ensure that every puppy we sell is of the highest quality.

Meet Our Team

Tresa & Clay Kimbrough

Founders of Three River Retrievers. Tresa and Clay have dedicated their lives to breeding healthy and happy Golden Retrievers. With years of experience in breeding and care, they ensure every puppy receives the best start in life.

Our Care Team

Our team is dedicated to providing the highest level of care for each puppy. From daily play sessions to health checkups, every puppy is treated like family during their time at Three River Retrievers.

Why Choose Us?

  • Responsible breeding practices that prioritize the health and well-being of our dogs.
  • Each puppy undergoes comprehensive health testing before being placed in a new home.
  • Our puppies are socialized from a young age to ensure they are happy and confident in their new homes.
  • We provide ongoing support and guidance to all of our puppy families.
